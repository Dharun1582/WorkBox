What is WORK BOX?

  • Work Box is a marketplace where employers and employees are able to find each other. The site allows employers to post work for site members who place bids in a competitive tender process.
  • Work Box establishes a global connection among professional minds.
  • Work Box allows anyone to post a work on this site, where other users bid on that project with their own budget. Finally the user who posted the work can choose the best among them and awards their project to that particular user.
  • After completion of the project, the user who posted that project is asked to pay the amount for it. he is then directed to the payment portal where the money transaction takes place.
